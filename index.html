Akan Name Generator

Welcome to the Akan Name Generator!
Akan names come from Ghanaian culture, where children are named based on the day they were born.

Ever heard of Kwame Nkrumah? Kwame is a name given to males born on a Saturday.
While Ama is a name given to girls born on a Saturday!

What about Koffi Annan? Koffi is a name given to males born on a Friday.
While Afua is a name given to girls born on a Friday!
